Overview

Canva AI 2.0 is Canva's largest product launch to date. It transforms the platform from a design tool into a full creative operating system — one that can research, plan, generate, brand-apply, schedule, and publish, all from a single conversation inside the tools you already use.

The nine capabilities below don't operate in isolation. They're designed to work together — each one removing a different bottleneck in the creation process.

The 9 Capabilities

01
Conversational Design
Design by describing your goal in plain language
+
What it is

The ability to design through plain language. You describe what you want to create — your goal, your audience, your rough idea — and Canva AI generates it. No blank page. No template search. No drag-and-drop from scratch.

How it works

You type a prompt on the Canva homepage or inside any existing design. Canva AI interprets your intent, selects appropriate layouts, elements, and copy, and builds a first draft. You continue the conversation to refine it.

Workflow impact
  • Removes the blank page problem entirely — first drafts start from a prompt, not a template
  • Non-designers on your team can self-serve on simple deliverables without briefing a designer
  • Design iteration moves from click-by-click editing to natural conversation
  • Works across all formats: social posts, presentations, documents, whiteboards
Try this

Open Canva and type: "Create an Instagram post announcing [your product/event] for [your audience]." Watch the first draft. Then prompt: "Make the headline more direct" or "Swap the background for something minimal." Stay in conversation.

02
Agentic Orchestration
AI takes actions on your behalf — decides, selects, executes
+
What it is

Canva AI can now take actions on your behalf — not just generate, but decide, select, and execute. It picks fonts, colours, layouts, and copy. It can start a new design from scratch or step into one you're already working on.

How it works

When you give Canva AI a goal, it acts like a creative director with execution ability — makes design decisions, pulls assets from the Canva library, applies brand settings, and iterates. You can interrupt at any point to redirect it.

Workflow impact
  • First drafts require less human setup — AI makes the initial design decisions
  • Mid-project, hand off specific tasks without starting over
  • Reduces back-and-forth between brief and execution — AI holds the brief in context
  • Frees designers to focus on judgment and refinement, not production mechanics
Try this

Open an existing design you're halfway through. Prompt: "This deck needs a stronger visual hierarchy. Rework the layout of slides 3 to 6." Let it execute. Review and redirect from there.

03
Memory Library
A personalised context layer that learns how you work
+
What it is

A personalised context layer that Canva AI builds from your existing designs, preferences, and working style. The first entry is your "About Me" — a Canva Doc generated by analysing your design history. It updates continuously as you work.

How it works

On first use, Canva AI scans your existing designs and creates an About Me profile. Over time, you add more entries: goals, preferences, a knowledge base, and shared memory with collaborators.

Workflow impact
  • Every new design starts with more context — less re-briefing the AI each time
  • Your style and preferences carry forward automatically across projects
  • Shared Memory means the AI can hold team context, not just individual context
  • The Knowledge Base lets you inject brand or product knowledge the AI doesn't have natively
Try this

Activate your About Me from the Canva homepage. Review what it generated. Add anything it missed — your industry, your typical audience, your design preferences. This is the context every future generation will pull from.

04
Layered Object Intelligence
Every AI output is a fully editable, layered Canva file
+
What it is

Every design Canva AI generates is fully layered and editable. Not a flattened image. Not a locked group. A proper, structured Canva file where every element — text, image, shape, icon — is individually selectable and editable.

How it works

Powered by Canva's in-house Design Model. When you open an AI-generated file, you see the same editable layers you'd get if you'd built it manually. Move, resize, restyle, or replace any element directly.

Workflow impact
  • AI outputs are actual working files, not starting points that need to be rebuilt
  • Designers can take AI-generated drafts and refine them without starting from scratch
  • Element-level control means you override specific parts, not the whole design
  • Connects to the full Canva toolkit: animations, brand assets, effects all work on AI-generated layers
Try this

Generate a design via prompt. Then open it and click into individual elements. Try moving the headline. Try swapping the background image. Confirm you have full layer access. This is your new baseline.

05
Connectors
Slack, Gmail, Google Drive, Calendar — all feed into designs
+
What it is

Direct integrations with the tools where your information already lives. Canva AI reads from these sources and uses the context to generate and populate designs without you having to manually move information across.

How it works

Connect your apps once in Canva settings. When you prompt a design, reference these sources: "Pull the latest updates from Slack and populate this newsletter template." Canva AI reads, extracts, and builds. Bi-directional connection is coming — designs will push context back to Slack and other tools.

Workflow impact
  • Eliminates the copy-paste loop between your comms tools and your design tool
  • Brief-to-design pipelines become much shorter — Slack thread to formatted post in one prompt
  • Context from live conversations feeds directly into content, keeping it current
  • Meeting notes, project updates, and announcements can auto-populate recurring templates
Try this

Connect Slack and Gmail in Canva settings. Then prompt: "Create a summary post of this week's team updates from Slack, using this brand template." This is your new weekly content workflow.

06
Scheduling
Recurring work runs automatically — even while you're offline
+
What it is

The ability to set tasks to run automatically on a defined cadence — even when you're offline. Canva AI executes the work on schedule and has it ready when you return.

How it works

After building a workflow, you set a schedule: daily, weekly, monthly. Canva AI runs the workflow, generates the output, and queues it for your review. Pairs with Connectors to pull fresh data each cycle.

Workflow impact
  • Recurring content — social posts, newsletters, internal updates — moves from manual to automated
  • Work doesn't stop when you stop — overnight research, Monday-morning content drops run without you
  • Reduces the cognitive load of remembering to produce recurring deliverables
  • Pairs with Connectors: automated workflows that pull from live sources and generate fresh content
Try this

Identify one recurring piece of content in your workflow — a weekly post, monthly newsletter, regular report. Build it once in Canva AI with Connectors active. Schedule it. Let it run once without you and review the output.

07
Web Research
Research happens inside the design, not in another tab
+
What it is

Canva AI can search the web and pull relevant information directly into your design — in context, without switching tabs. No more research phase separate from the design phase.

How it works

Within a design workflow, prompt Canva AI to research a topic, a client, a market, or a news event. It queries the web, synthesises results, and injects relevant information into the appropriate part of your design. Research doesn't break the creative flow.

Workflow impact
  • Research and design happen in the same conversation — no more tab-switching mid-build
  • Client-facing materials can be refreshed with current data on demand
  • Pitch decks, reports, and proposals can include live, sourced information without manual lookup
  • Reduces the time between "I need to check something" and "it's in the design"
Try this

Build a pitch deck for a real client. At the relevant slide, prompt: "Find [client]'s latest news and add a summary to this slide." Then: "Check for any recent industry developments that affect this pitch." This replaces your pre-meeting research sprint.

08
Brand Intelligence
On-brand by default — from the very first draft
+
What it is

Canva AI automatically applies your Brand Kit to every design it generates — from the first draft. Correct fonts, correct colours, correct logo, correct assets. On-brand by default, not by revision.

How it works

Set up your Brand Kit in Canva. When Brand Intelligence is active, every AI generation starts from that kit. Select a Brand Template and the AI populates it on-brand. Charts and new additions auto-match the established palette. Affinity (now free for all Canva users) assets can be pushed directly into Brand Kits.

Workflow impact
  • First drafts are on-brand — the feedback loop of "wrong fonts, wrong colours" disappears
  • Non-designers can generate content without breaking visual standards
  • Brand consistency scales across a team without designer oversight on every output
  • Affinity integration means high-fidelity brand assets flow into templates seamlessly
Try this

Audit your current Canva Brand Kit. Make sure it's complete: primary and secondary fonts, full colour palette, logo variations, and at least one Brand Template per content type you produce regularly. Brand Intelligence is only as strong as what you put in.

09
Canva Code 2.0
Interactive HTML tools and microsites — no coding required
+
What it is

The ability to import, edit, and create HTML and interactive code inside Canva — without writing code. AI-generated HTML files become drag-and-drop editable. Interactive elements can be built by prompt and published directly to a domain.

How it works

Built in collaboration with Anthropic and OpenAI. Import any HTML file into Canva — it becomes editable in the drag-and-drop editor. Build interactive elements from scratch by describing them: RSVP forms, polls, countdowns. Form responses feed into a connected Canva Sheet. Publish to your own domain in one click.

Workflow impact
  • Microsites, landing pages, and interactive tools are now inside your design workflow — no developer required
  • HTML imports mean AI-generated code from external tools can be refined inside Canva without rebuilding
  • Form + Sheet integration creates a light data collection pipeline without third-party tools
  • Interactive presentations, audience polls, and event tools can be built and published without leaving Canva
Try this

Describe a simple interactive element you currently use a separate tool for — a contact form, an event RSVP, a quick poll. Prompt Canva Code 2.0 to build it. Publish it to a domain and test the Sheet integration.

How It All Connects

These nine capabilities are designed to work as a system. Here is how they compound in a real workflow:

1
Connectors

Pull context from Slack, Gmail, or Drive — so the AI knows what it's working with before it generates anything.

2
Conversational Design + Agentic Orchestration

Turn that context into a first draft — layered, structured, ready to edit.

3
Brand Intelligence

Applies your Brand Kit from the first generation — no manual correction needed.

4
Web Research

Fills in any gaps — client data, market context, current news — without leaving the design.

5
Layered Object Intelligence

Means the output is a real editable file — every element accessible and modifiable.

6
Memory Library

Holds your preferences and style — so every subsequent generation starts smarter.

7
Scheduling

Runs the whole workflow on repeat — weekly, monthly, automatically — while you focus on other things.
